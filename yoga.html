yoga teaching

Come home
to your body.

My teaching is rooted in the belief that yoga is not about flexibility or performance — it is about presence. Each class is a space to arrive, to breathe, and to remember who you are beneath the noise.

Philosophy

How I teach

○

Grounded Presence

We begin in the body, not the mind. Every session opens with stillness so you can sense what you actually need today.

∿

Intentional Movement

Alignment is a conversation, not a commandment. I offer clear cues and invite you to explore what feels true in your own body.

◆

Breathwork First

The breath is the thread that runs through every practice — it guides pace, depth, and the quality of your attention.

◦

Rest as Practice

Savasana is not a reward for hard work. Restoration is woven into every class because stillness is where the practice integrates.

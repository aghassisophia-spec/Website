About me

I am a teacher, a maker of images, and a student of slow living.

Hi, I'm Sophia. I'm a yoga teacher, photographer, and personal brand guide based in [your city / region]. My work lives at the intersection of movement, beauty, and intentional living.

I came to yoga after [a period of burnout / a need to reconnect / your own story]. What started as a way to feel better in my body became a full philosophy — one that now shapes how I move, how I see, and how I help others build their own practices and businesses.

My photography grew out of a desire to document the textures of everyday life — morning light through linen, hands cupped around a warm mug, the soft pause between breath and movement. I bring that same unhurried attention to every shoot.

When I'm not on the mat or behind a lens, you'll find me reading in the garden, cooking something slow, or writing on my Substack.
